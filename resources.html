Study Tools

Here are some useful study tools that can help you better understand cybersecurity topics:

  • Cybrary - Offers free and paid cybersecurity courses.
  • Udemy Cybersecurity Courses - A variety of courses on cybersecurity basics and advanced topics.
  • Codecademy: Computer Science - Learn the basics of programming and security concepts.

Outside Resources

These external resources provide additional learning materials on cybersecurity topics:

  • SANS Cybersecurity Courses - Offers in-depth courses and certifications for cybersecurity professionals.
  • Coursera Cybersecurity Courses - University-backed cybersecurity courses.
  • NIST Cybersecurity Framework - Learn about the National Institute of Standards and Technology's guidelines for cybersecurity.

MTU Resources

Michigan Technological University (MTU) offers a variety of resources related to cybersecurity:

  • MTU Cybersecurity Programs - Learn about the cybersecurity degree programs offered at MTU.
  • MTU Student Resources - A list of resources available to MTU students to help them get started in cybersecurity.
  • MTU Cybersecurity Club - Join the MTU Cybersecurity Club to participate in events and gain hands-on experience.
